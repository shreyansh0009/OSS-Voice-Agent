# Godrej Appliances — Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Overview
Godrej Appliances is a business unit of Godrej & Boyce, the flagship company of the Godrej Enterprises Group. Established in 1958 as the **first Indian company to manufacture refrigerators**, Godrej Appliances has expanded into one of India's leading home appliance brands — with a portfolio of **350+ SKUs** across multiple categories, powered by the philosophy of **"Things Made Thoughtfully."**

The brand serves individual consumers, housing societies, commercial establishments, and healthcare institutions across India through **740+ service centers** and **4,500+ SmartBuddy service experts** pan-India.

## Support Features (Ready to Handle)

### Complaint & Service Management
- Inbound complaint registration (product malfunction, no cooling, noise, leakage, etc.)
- Service Request Number (SRN) generation with 3-digit CSN
- Service engineer dispatch within 24 hours (or same day if before 6:30 PM)
- 740+ service centers across India
- 4,500+ SmartBuddy field service experts
- Post-service follow-up and satisfaction survey
- Repeat complaint escalation to senior support
- Safety emergency handling (smoke, electrical shock, gas leakage — highest priority)

### Installation & Setup
- New product installation booking (AC, washing machine, dishwasher)
- Installation by Godrej-authorized technicians
- Relocation service (product moved to new address)
- Post-installation checklist and demo

### Warranty & Claims Management
- Standard manufacturer warranty verification by product and purchase date
- Extended Warranty Program (up to 5 years, cashless service)
- AC Comprehensive Warranty: 5 years free (purchase from Sept 1, 2024 onwards)
  - Coverage: Labour + all functional parts — gas charging, compressor, inverter drive, remote, PCB, fan motors, heat exchange coils
  - Compressor: 10-year warranty
  - Registration required within 7 days of purchase (from Aug 1, 2025 purchases: within 30 days)
- Refrigerator & Washing Machine Extended Warranty programs (1-year extensions available)
- Deep Freezer: 5-year warranty
- Warranty validation by authorized technician on-site

### Sales & Product Inquiry Support
- Product feature and specification guidance
- Product comparison assistance
- Pricing guidance (directed to authorized dealer or website)
- Dealer locator by city / pincode
- EMI and financing options (via partner banks and dealers)
- Festive and seasonal offer information
- New purchase transition: installation booking, warranty registration

### Escalation Management
- Three-stage de-escalation for angry customers (Calm → Warning → Transfer)
- Engineer no-show re-booking with priority assignment
- Repeat complainant high-priority flagging
- Legal threat / consumer forum escalation to senior management
- Social media complaint urgency escalation
