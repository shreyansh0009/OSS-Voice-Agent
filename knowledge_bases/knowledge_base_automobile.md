## Overview
CRM Landing delivers comprehensive Salesforce solutions for the entire automotive value chain — from OEMs to Dealers to end Customers. Their automotive solutions cover 2W, 3W, 4W, electric vehicles (EVs), and commercial vehicles.

## Automotive Customer Types Served
- OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers): 2W, 3W, 4W, Bicycle manufacturers
- Component Manufacturers: Auto lighting, brakes, electricals, train wagons, trailers, helmets, bicycle components
- Captive Functions: Finance, Insurance, IT arms of OEMs
- Aggregators: Leasing, service, used vehicle platforms, ride hailing, truck aggregators
- Manufacturers: Machine manufacturers producing for component manufacturers
- Dealers: Large dealers servicing multiple brands
