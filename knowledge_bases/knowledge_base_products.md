# Greaves Mobility — Products Knowledge Base

## Ampere Primus (Premium Electric Scooter)

### Overview
The flagship electric scooter from Greaves Mobility. Designed for daily commuters who want the best range, performance, and features.

### Key Specifications
- Range: Up to 120 km per charge (Eco mode)
- Top Speed: 60 km/h
- Motor: 2.2 kW BLDC motor
- Battery: 2.3 kWh lithium-ion (removable)
- Charging Time: 5 hours (home charger), 2 hours (fast charger)
- Payload: 150 kg
- Ground Clearance: 155 mm

### Features
- LED headlamp and tail lamp
- Digital instrument cluster with Bluetooth connectivity
- Mobile app integration (ride stats, charging status, locate vehicle)
- Regenerative braking
- Alloy wheels
- Under-seat storage: 22 liters
- USB charging port
- Anti-theft alarm
- Reverse mode

### Colors Available
- Glossy Red, Matte Black, Pearl White, Cosmic Blue

### Best For
- Daily office commuters (20-50 km round trip)
- Premium buyers who want the best range and features
- Tech-savvy riders who want app connectivity

- Motor: 1.2 kW BLDC motor
