# Greaves Mobility — Company Knowledge Base

## Company Overview
Greaves Mobility is one of India's leading electric vehicle manufacturers. Part of Greaves Cotton Limited (established 1859), the company manufactures electric two-wheelers and three-wheelers under the Ampere brand. Headquartered in Bangalore, India.

Mission: Making electric mobility accessible, affordable, and reliable for every Indian.

## Electric Vehicle Products

### Electric Scooters (Ampere Brand)
- **Ampere Primus** — Premium electric scooter. Best-in-class range (up to 120 km per charge). Ideal for daily commuters who need long range and premium features. LED display, alloy wheels, regenerative braking.
- **Magnus EX** — Family-friendly electric scooter. Comfortable ride with good storage. Range up to 100 km. Wide floorboard, comfortable seat for two.
- **Zeal EX** — Budget-friendly city commuter. Range up to 90 km. Lightweight, easy to handle. Best value for money.

### Electric Three-Wheelers
- Greaves also manufactures electric three-wheelers for last-mile delivery and passenger transport.

## Battery & Charging

### Battery Specifications
- Lithium-ion battery packs (removable on select models)
- Range: 90–120 km per full charge depending on model, riding style, terrain, and load
- Battery life: designed for 1000+ charge cycles

### Charging Options
- **Home Charger**: Standard charger included with every vehicle. 4–5 hours for full charge. Plugs into regular 15A socket.
- **Fast Charger**: ~2 hours for full charge. Available at Greaves fast-charging stations.
- **Charging Network**: 50+ fast-charging locations across India, expanding rapidly.
- **Portable Charger**: Can charge anywhere with a standard power outlet.

### Battery Care Tips
- Avoid complete discharge — charge when battery reaches 20%
- Store in cool, dry place
- Use only Greaves-approved chargers
- Optimal charging temperature: 10°C to 45°C

## Warranty

### Standard Warranty
- **Battery**: 3 years or 30,000 km (whichever comes first)
- **Motor & Controller**: 2 years standard warranty
- **Vehicle Frame**: 5 years

### Extended Warranty
- Available for purchase at the time of buying or within first year
- Covers battery, motor, and controller for additional years

### What's NOT Covered
- Physical damage, water submersion beyond IP rating, unauthorized modifications, wear items (tires, brakes)
